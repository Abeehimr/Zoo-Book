About Kids Animals

A tiny site for kids to explore and learn friendly animal facts.

Our Mission

Make learning light, friendly, and safe for kids. Simple pages, big buttons, and happy colors.

Design Patterns

  • Master–Detail: Browse animals and see details side-by-side.
  • Wizard: Multi-step quiz with Back/Next and a finish summary.
  • Modal: Final results shown in a focus-trapped dialog.

Accessibility

  • Semantic HTML and ARIA labels.
  • Keyboard-friendly navigation.
  • High-contrast and readable fonts.

Tech Stack

  • Pure HTML/CSS with light JavaScript for progressive enhancement.
  • No frameworks to keep it simple and fast.
  • Responsive layout with CSS Grid and Flexbox.

Team

We are a small group of designers and developers who love building playful learning tools.

Contact

Questions or feedback? Email us at hello@example.com.

Privacy for Kids

We do not collect personal data. This site runs fully on your device.

Learning Tips

  • Explore each animal and read the fun facts.
  • Try the quiz and talk about the answers with a parent or teacher.
  • Repeat the quiz to remember better — practice makes perfect!
